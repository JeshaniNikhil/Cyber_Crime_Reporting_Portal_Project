About App Testing

App Testing Services

At Cyber Armor, we offer comprehensive app testing services to ensure your mobile applications function flawlessly across all devices and platforms. Our expert team is skilled in various types of mobile app testing, ensuring your app delivers the best user experience and performance.

App Testing Types

Manual Testing

Thorough testing of the app manually by our experts to identify bugs, usability issues, and overall app performance. This includes executing test cases without automation tools.

Automated Testing

Utilizes automation tools to perform repetitive tasks and regression testing efficiently. It ensures the app's functionality and performance remain consistent with every new update.

Usability Testing

Focuses on evaluating the app's user interface (UI) and user experience (UX). We assess how easy and intuitive your app is for end-users, aiming to enhance user satisfaction.

Performance Testing

Measures the app's speed, responsiveness, and stability under various conditions. We test for load times, stress handling, and overall performance to ensure optimal user experienc

Security Testing

Identifies vulnerabilities and ensures that your app is protected against potential threats. Our team checks for data security, privacy issues, and compliance with security standards.

Compatibility Testing

Verifies that your app works seamlessly across different devices, operating systems, and network environments. This includes testing on various screen sizes, hardware configurations, and OS versions.

Installation Testing

Ensures the app installs, updates, and uninstalls correctly without issues. We test different installation scenarios to ensure a smooth user experience from the start.

Interrupt Testing

Tests how the app handles interruptions such as incoming calls, messages, low battery alerts, and other notifications. This ensures the app maintains its state and performance during interruptions.
